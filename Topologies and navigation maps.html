What is a Topology?

LANs vs WANs: LAN (Local Area Network) connects devices within a small, private area like a home or office, offering high speeds. WAN (Wide Area Network) spans large geographical areas, connecting multiple LANs across cities or countries, with the internet being the prime example. LANs are for internal sharing; WANs are for external connectivity.

Client-Server Model: The client-server model is a distributed application structure that partitions tasks between service providers (servers) and service requesters (clients), typically over a network. Clients initiate requests to servers, which process and respond to those requests, enabling functionalities like web browsing, email, and database access.

Bus Topology: In a bus topology, all devices are connected to a single communication line called the bus. Data is transmitted along the bus, and each device listens for data addressed to it.

Star Topology: In a star topology, all devices are connected to a central hub or switch. Data is transmitted from the central hub to the devices, and each device communicates with the hub rather than directly with each other.

Ring Topology: In a ring topology, each device is connected to two other devices, forming a circular data path. Data travels in one direction around the ring until it reaches its destination.

Mesh Topology: In a mesh topology, each device is connected to every other device in the network. This provides high redundancy and reliability, as data can take multiple paths to reach its destination.

Hybrid Topology: A hybrid topology combines two or more different types of topologies, such as star and bus, to create a more flexible and scalable network design that can meet specific requirements.

Navigation Maps: Navigation maps in networking refer to the visual representation of a network's structure, showing how devices are interconnected and how data flows between them. These maps help network administrators understand the layout of the network, identify potential bottlenecks, and troubleshoot issues effectively.
